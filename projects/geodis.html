About my role in this project and my global feeling

I started my journey at GEODIS as both Level L2/L3 Analyst for Identity and Access Management (IAM) & HR, as well as a UX Designer between November 2018–October 2022. As the principal UX Designer, I faced various challenges:

  • ✅ Junior UX Designer: While I had a strong foundation from BoursoBank, it was a learning curve in a new domain.
  • ✅ New domain: I had limited experience with LDAP and HR systems before joining GEODIS.
  • ✅ Understanding the scope: Grasping the interaction between IAM & HR data required constant learning and iteration.

Today, I am both an expert in IAM & HR data management and a skilled UX Designer. The "Office365 Account Provisioning" project was designed during the COVID‑19 pandemic (Mar–Sept 2020), followed through dev Oct 2020–Jan 2021, first deployed in France Mar 2021 and rolled out worldwide by 2025–2026.

Problem Statement

We identified issues with data quality in Office 365 directories. Admins entered incorrect data, licensing costs were uncontrolled, and GEODIS' IT‑HR ecosystem was fragmented. Our goal: streamline Office 365 account provisioning, automate the process, and sync data directly from the HR system.

Previous Tool: AD Admin Interface

Challenges & Context

Before our solution, HR–IT account sync was manual via email, leading to inefficiencies and poor traceability. Processes differed by region. Our approach centralized account creation, license management, and data validation in one platform.

User Research & Needs Gathering

We conducted qualitative research to understand daily challenges of Office365 admins, HR managers, and team leads. Methods included one-on-one interviews, collaborative workshops, surveys, and process audits.

  • ✅ User Interviews with admins and HR managers.
  • ✅ Collaborative Workshops with stakeholders to co-create and prioritize features.
  • ✅ Surveys for broader insights.
  • ✅ Process Audit to uncover inefficiencies.

Key takeaways:

  • ✅ Manual processes were long and error-prone.
  • ✅ Lack of HR system integration caused inconsistencies.
  • ✅ High error rate due to missing validation.
  • ✅ Clear need for automation in provisioning and license management.
